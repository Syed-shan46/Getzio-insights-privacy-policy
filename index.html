2. Privacy Policy

This policy details how we collect, process, and secure your credentials and workspace data while using Getzio Insights.

A. Information We Collect

  • Authentication Data: Your phone number is securely processed via Firebase Authentication to authenticate your device and send secure one-time passcode (OTP) verification codes.
  • Operational Analytics Metrics: All sales data, active user figures, team performance indicators, and database statuses are securely read and cached locally on your device in a sandboxed, encrypted Hive storage layer.
  • Telemetry & Diagnostics: Technical statistics such as your device OS version (iOS/Android), application crash logs, and network API latency metrics are processed to optimize backend server efficiency.

B. Core Data Utilization

We process your data strictly to enable features, maintain security, and optimize server health:

  • Secure Identity Verification: Resolving OTP log-ins and maintaining your encrypted token.
  • Real-Time Dashboards: Caching local database summaries for immediate loading.
  • AI Business Predictions: Generating future predictive growth trends within the client interface.
